About Me

I'm Reshma Mathew. I'm a post graduate in computer applications. During my work experience I came across web designing and dound it very interesting. I've joined Humber with the intention to learn and understand web development standards used internationally. Also, I look forward to learning every phase of development of a website i.e., from design to coding to integration to a database.

Talking about myself, I'm a very optimistic person and I stand by my faith in God. I like to hang around with friends and meet up with my cousins once in a while. I love to enjoy my food, seafood and chicken cooked in Indian style with spices are my favourite. My favorite pass time for the past few months has been browsing about various web designing topics on the internet.
